Project Overview

QR is an innovative mobile application developed by Corner TwentySix (Amara Media), an organization dedicated to providing cutting-edge solutions.

Specializing as a powerful QR code scanning tool, QR enables users to effortlessly scan QR codes in diverse locations. By scanning QR codes placed in key locations, users can quickly access local history and nearby recommendations.QR also provides a convenient way to save scanned locations for future reference or share them with others.

With its user-friendly interface and comprehensive features, QR enhances the experience of discovering and accessing information about places of interest.

The Problem

When people travel to new places, they often face challenges in finding information about those locations. This can lead to frustrations when they rely solely on Google searches to discover where to stay, nearby popular attractions, activities, and restaurants.

These challenges can significantly impact the traveler's experience, potentially dampening their excitement and enjoyment of exploring new destinations.

The Solution

We have developed an application designed to address all of these challenges and provide a comprehensive solution for users. Our application aims to simplify the process of finding information about new places, offering users a seamless experience to discover accommodations, nearby attractions, activities, and dining options.

With our app, users can enhance their travel experience and eliminate the frustrations commonly associated with exploring unfamiliar destinations.

Project Overview

BAS Self Service is an innovative attendance application developed by Intercorp Solutions Pte Ltd, an organization dedicated to providing advanced business solutions.

Specializing in attendance management, BAS Self Service enables users to easily check in and check out, apply for and manage leave, as well as submit and manage claims. With a user-friendly interface, the application streamlines attendance processes and improves overall efficiency.

By offering comprehensive features and seamless functionality, BAS Self Service enhances the experience of managing attendance, leave applications, and claims for both employees and administrators.

Problem Statement

When employees manage their attendance, leaves, and claims, they often face challenges navigating different systems or manually tracking these processes. This can lead to frustrations, as employees may struggle to efficiently check in and out, apply for leave, or submit claims, while administrators face difficulties in managing approvals and tracking attendance data.

These challenges can significantly impact the overall efficiency of workforce management, potentially leading to decreased productivity and dissatisfaction among both employees and administrators.

The Solution

We have developed an application designed to address all of these challenges and provide a comprehensive solution for workforce management.
BAS Self Service simplifies the process of checking in and out, applying for and managing leave, and submitting and managing claims.

With this app, users can efficiently handle attendance and leave-related tasks while administrators gain a seamless tool for managing approvals and tracking data. BAS Self Service eliminates the frustrations commonly associated with manual tracking, offering a streamlined, user-friendly experience for both employees and administrators.

Project Overview

identi.ly is a state-of-the-art web application developed by Identily Inc., a leader in secure digital solutions committed to delivering cutting-edge technology.

identi.ly app provides a secure, user-friendly platform for encrypted file transfer, utilizing facial recognition (face key) for both encryption and decryption, ensuring maximum security and privacy for its users.

Problem Statement

When individuals and businesses need to transfer sensitive files, they often face challenges ensuring their data remains secure. Relying on traditional file sharing methods or complex encryption tools can lead to frustration, as they may expose users to potential security risks or require a high level of technical expertise.

These challenges can negatively impact the user experience, creating concerns about data breaches and complicating the process of sharing important information.

The Solution

We have developed an application designed to address all of these challenges and provide a comprehensive solution for secure file transfers.
identi.ly simplifies the process of encrypting and sharing sensitive files, using facial recognition technology (face key) to ensure only authorized users can access the data.

With identi.ly, individuals and businesses can transfer files confidently, eliminating the frustrations and risks associated with traditional methods and complex encryption tools, while ensuring the highest level of security and privacy.
